03 : About me

Early Life and Schools

I, Asuama Yeboah Atoapoma, embarked on my academic journey at the University of Ghana, where I pursued a BA in Administration, specializing in Finance and Economics. My passion for finance led me to acquire several professional qualifications, including Chartered Accountant from the Institute of Chartered Accountants, Ghana (ICAG), Investment Foundation Certificate from the Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) Institute, Professional Banking from the Chartered Institute Of Bankers (CIB-GH), and Qualified Chartered Tax Practitioner from the Chartered Institute of Taxation Ghana. To further my knowledge, I pursued an MSc in International Business from the University of Ghana and an MSc in Financial Engineering from WorldQuant University.

Works, Activities, and Projects

I have accumulated valuable experience in various roles, contributing to the financial sector and technology industry. As a Financial Analyst at PayInc Group - FinTech in London, I analyzed business and financial data, performed forecasting, and collaborated closely with the accounting team for accurate reporting. At Maphlix Trust Ghana Ltd, a multinational corporation, I served as the Finance Manager, formulating financial policies, ensuring compliance, and contributing to effective decision-making.

Additionally, I managed day-to-day accounting operations efficiently as an Accountant at GTi Group, implementing internal controls and liaising with subsidiaries for timely finance information. My involvement in various projects, such as KPMG's Data Analytics Consulting and an internship at the Ministry of Finance and Economic Planning, showcases my skills in data analysis and financial management.

Outside the corporate world, I have engaged in voluntary teaching and entrepreneurship, establishing ventures in fast-food and agriculture. Recognized for my achievements as one of the youngest Chartered Accountants in Ghana, I continue to explore AI-driven solutions in finance and agriculture.

Looking ahead, my future projects will focus on advancing AI applications in agriculture and finance, bringing transformative solutions to the industry. I possess a diverse skill set, including proficiency in various programming languages and strong interpersonal skills.

Vision

My vision is to transform the finance, accounting, and tax sectors by leveraging the power of generative AI. Over the next three years, I plan to develop and implement AI solutions that can automate and optimize various financial processes, thereby increasing efficiency and reducing errors. This will not only revolutionize the way these sectors operate but also free up human resources to focus on more strategic and creative tasks. I believe that with the right application of AI, we can unlock unprecedented opportunities in these sectors. Stay tuned for updates on future projects!
